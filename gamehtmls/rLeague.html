Rocket League

Rocket League is a high-powered hybrid of arcade-style soccer and vehicular
mayhem with easy-to-understand controls and fluid, physics-driven competition.
Rocket League includes casual and competitive Online Matches, a fully-featured
offline Season Mode, special “Mutators” that let you change the rules entirely,
hockey and basketball-inspired Extra Modes, and more than 500 trillion possible
cosmetic customization combinations.
